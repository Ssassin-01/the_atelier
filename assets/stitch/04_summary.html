Pumpkin Porridge Dessert

* final plating idea

1. Pumpkin Cream

{
  • Roasted Pumpkin 250g
  • Heavy Cream 150g
  • Brown Sugar 35g
  • Cinnamon 1 pinch
}

Roast pumpkin at 180C until completely soft. Puree while hot. In a cold bowl, whip heavy cream with sugar and cinnamon until soft peaks form. Gently fold puree into the cream. *Do not overmix, keep it airy! Chill for 2 hours before piping.

2. Mini Rice Ball (Mochi)

{
  • Glutinous Rice Flour 100g
  • Warm Water ~80ml
  • Sugar 10g
}

Mix flour and sugar. Slowly add warm water until dough comes together. Knead until smooth. Roll into tiny spheres (approx 5g each). Boil until they float, then plunge immediately into ice water to keep them chewy.

3. Pumpkin Seed Tuile

{
  • Butter (melted) 40g
  • Icing Sugar 40g
  • Egg White 40g
  • Flour 30g
  • Pumpkin Seeds 50g
}

Whisk egg whites and icing sugar. Stir in flour, then melted butter. Fold in lightly toasted pumpkin seeds. Let rest for 30 mins. Spread thinly on silpat. Bake at 160C for 8-10 mins until golden. *Must shape immediately while hot!

4. Rice Crumble

{
  • Rice Flour 50g
  • Almond Flour 50g
  • Cold Butter 50g
  • Demerara Sugar 40g
  • Salt 1 pinch
}

Combine all dry ingredients. Cut in cold butter until coarse crumbs form. Spread on a baking sheet. Bake at 170C for 15 mins, tossing halfway through, until deeply golden and fragrant. Cool completely for crunch.
